Plastering

You can put your trust in our time served plasterers with over 20 years experience in the plastering business. Based in the city centre, we offer plastering services in Manchester and its surrounding areas. We specialise in jobs of all sizes, no matter if you’re looking for a small home improvement job or you’re a contractor seeking commercial plastering services.

TV Wall Mounting Service

We are a team that offers professional TV Wall Mounting Services for your OLED, QLED, LED & LCD 4K Smart TV’s.
